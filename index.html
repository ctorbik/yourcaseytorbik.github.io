About me

Where strategy meets
something genuine

I recently graduated magna cum laude from Northeastern University's D'Amore-McKim School of Business with a B.S. in Business Administration and Communications, concentrating in Marketing with a minor in Consulting. I also spent a summer studying Brand Management at the London School of Economics.

Most recently, I was on the Samuel Adams Brand Team at The Boston Beer Company — developing social content, crafting creative briefs with senior leadership, and conducting competitive research that directly informed new product development. Before that, I completed a Marketing Co-op at CDM Smith, where I wrote proposals, managed databases, and contributed to business development strategy.

I've also served as a Peer Mentor and Teaching Assistant for Northeastern's co-op program, supporting 40+ students through resume reviews, mock interviews, and professional development. I'm currently looking for my next role in marketing, brand management, or advertising.
